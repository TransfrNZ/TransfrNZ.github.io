How to send money with Transfr

1

Create an account

Sign up with your email, create a unique username and link your bank account.

2

Find your mates

Enter their username or pick from your friends list. If they're not on the app, invite them!

3

Choose the amount and send!

Enter the amount, click send, and that's it! The recipient will receive a notification you have made a payment.
